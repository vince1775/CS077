Important: How In-Class Points Work
In-class Assignment(ICA) points represent 1 point for attendance and participation.

ICA's are required for receiving full credit on the weekly assignment.

ICA's are guided and due the same day of instructions.
Missing an in-class point automatically reduces that week’s assignment grade by 50%.
Missing more than 2 ICA will result in automatic drop from the course in the first 4 weeks of class.
